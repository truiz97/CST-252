Our Task

We decided to pseudocode wordle. It was one of the first games that came to my mind when listening to the lecture in class. Wordle is a simple web-based word game where you try to guess the correct 5 letter word.

    What actions can I take?

  • Come up with a 5 letter word and input it.

    • What do I need to remember or keep track of?

  • If the letters you input are marked green yellow or gray.

    • What do I do over and over again?

  • Inputting 5 letter words.

    • When do I need to make decisions?
